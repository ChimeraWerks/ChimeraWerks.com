# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

Senior engineers, engineering leads, and technical founders evaluating multi-agent orchestration tooling.
They arrive skeptical, allergic to marketing fog, and fluent in the failure modes the product fixes (babysitting terminals, glue scripts, swallowed stderr).
Secondary: prospective consulting clients of Chimera Werks who need to read "serious engineers" within the first viewport.

## Product Purpose

ChimeraWerks.com is the umbrella site for the Chimera Werks ecosystem, with Chimera Relay as the flagship.
It exists to state the thesis (run a fleet of coding agents as one system), show the instruments (rooms, Chimera DNA run language, preserved error trails), and open a contact channel.
Success: a technical visitor understands the fleet-as-one-organism argument in one scroll and believes the people behind it ship real systems.

## Brand Personality

Cinematic, exacting, alive.
The dark 3D workshop world executed to studio craft: kinetic, atmospheric, and precise, never clean-corporate or stripped.
Three registers, in order: workshop (werks, bench, instruments), biology (genomes, strands, fusion, one organism), and exactly one beast moment (the faceted chimera mark).
Copy is systems biology stated literally, never fantasy-as-mechanic: genomes fuse, strands graft, one organism works.
Banned vocabulary: "swarm", "summon", "arcane", "magical".

## Anti-references

- Clean/light product-site minimalism (Linear, Vercel, Stripe clones). Tried twice, rejected twice: for this brand it reads as abandoning the world, not professionalizing it.
- Literal moody AI-generated mythology illustration (engraved beast plates, "the specimen", occult lore tags). This was the "talented amateur" tell; the myth layer is one crafted mark, nothing more.
- HUD theater: boot loaders, chapter counters, frame ornaments that decorate without arguing the product.
- The SaaS hero-metric template and identical icon-card grids.
- Generic biotech/genomics branding drift: the biology must always be visibly about coding agents, not lab science.

## Design Principles

1. The aesthetic is the argument. Every element must argue the product thesis (many genomes, one organism); if it only decorates, cut it.
2. Biology leads, the beast lands. DNA, strands, and fusion carry the explanation; the faceted chimera mark is the single myth artifact the science resolves into.
3. Elevate the world, don't strip it. "Professional" means craft and restraint applied to the dark cinematic 3D world: tight type, one accent system, strict spacing, real WebGL.
4. Instruments, not decoration. Product moments (the Relay console, DNA runs, error trails) are rendered as crisp, literal instrument panels a practitioner would recognize.
5. The trail stays intact. Copy is honest and technical, names real failure modes, and never hides behind abstraction.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG 2.1 AA contrast on all rendered text (ink-3 floor is tuned to 4.9:1 on surface-1; keep it there).
- Reduced motion is a first-class experience: every canvas layer gates on prefers-reduced-motion, so the static chimera mark fallback must always ship alongside the 3D world.
- Dark-only theme is deliberate (color-scheme: dark declared); legibility pockets keep copy readable over live 3D.
- All figures and diagrams carry meaningful roles/labels; decorative layers are aria-hidden.
